    Deployment Strategies

    The best deployment strategy for your environment depends on a few factors. Whether you want to monitor containers, the orchestration service you’re running, and the amount of automation you already have, there are a few routes to go.

    An abundance of containers

    To monitor containers across your fleet, you can use the native Kubernetes support. This comes packaged suitable for your environment and there’s no need to install drivers or kernel modules if you’re on one of the big cloud providers.

    A fleet of VMs

    To provision InGRAINd as part of your VM fleet, or Packer images, you’re best off taking inspiration from the Ansible rules, or using the integrations.

    Ansible integrates neatly with both Packer and Terraform through their modular environments.
